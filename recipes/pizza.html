Pizza

Description:

This easy to make pizza is the perfect go-to recipe. With a simple dough base, a layer or tomato sauce, and plenty
of cheese, it bakes golden in just a few minutes. Whether you use store-bought dough or make your own, this pizza
delivers delicious flavor with minimal effort.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Dried oragano

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and lightly grease a baking sheet or pizza pan.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and place it on the pan.
  3. Spread a thin, even layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  5. Drizzle a little olive oil on top and add optional seasonings like oregano or basil.
  6. Bake for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve.
