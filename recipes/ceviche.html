Ceviche

Description:

This easy ceviche is a fresh and flavorful dish that’s perfect for warm days, quick lunches, or casual gatherings.
Made with tender pieces of fresh shrimp marinated in citrus juice, it combines bright, zesty flavors with crisp
vegetables like tomatoes, onions, and peppers for a light yet satisfying bite. Fresh cilantro adds a burst of
herbaceous flavor, while the citrus creates the classic refreshing taste ceviche is known for. With simple
ingredients, minimal prep, and no complicated cooking involved, this recipe is an easy way to bring vibrant coastal
-inspired flavors to your table.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh shrimp
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Lemon juice
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeño
  • Salt and pepper
  • Avocado
  • Tortillas

Steps:

  1. Cut the fresh shrimp into small bite sized pieces and place them in a glass.
  2. Pour enough fresh lime juice over the fish to fully cover it.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for 20- 30 min, or until the fish turns opaque and looks cooked by the citrus.
  4. Drain a small amount of the citrus juice if desired.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes, chopped red onions, cliantro, and jalapeño to the bowl.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then gently mix everything together
  7. Fold in diced avocado if using.
  8. Serve chilled with tortilla chips.
